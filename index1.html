Top 5 most expensive NFT

5. Stay Free

The only NFT to date created by Edward Snowden. This is a collage from the text of a court decision that illegalized mass surveillance by the US National Security Agency.NFT was created using open source software. Value at the time of sale – $5.26 million

4. Crossroads

4. Crossroads: $ 6.6 million Created by an artist working under the pseudonym Beeple, this work of art demonstrates the author's cult style - irony and sarcasm towards public figures. Crossroads was created in response to the US presidential election. The video in it had to change depending on the election results (current image or Trump's winner).

3. CryptoPunk №7804

3. CryptoPunk №7804: $ 7.57 million This cryptopunk is one of nine Alien Punks. It was sold in March for only $ 10,000 cheaper than the most expensive cryptopunk (it goes further in the rankings).

2. CryptoPunk №3100

It is the most expensive cryptopunk, as well as one of the nine Alien Punks. It went a long way from the first price of $ 76 in 2017 to the final sale of $ 7.58 million in March earlier this year.

1. Everydays: The First 5000 Days

Christie’s world-renowned auction house and artist Mike Winkelmann, known as the Beeple, entered the history of non-reciprocal tokens (NFT) in late February, when the house left its first NFT auction in history without any accompanying physical works of art. Within minutes, $ 1 million soared and finally sold for $ 69 million. The work is called "Every day: the first 5000 days". This is a collage of 5,000 images for each day of May 2007.
